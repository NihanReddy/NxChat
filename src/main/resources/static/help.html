Help Center & FAQ

How do I create a new group?

To create a group, navigate to your chat list and click the "Create New Group" button at the bottom. Select the contacts you wish to add and provide a group name.

How can I switch to Light Mode?

Click the "Display Mode" toggle in the bottom left corner of your sidebar to switch between Dark and Light themes instantly.

Is my data secure?

Yes, all transmissions are handled securely. Your messages and contacts are encrypted in transit and securely verified using enterprise-grade authentication.

How do I sync my Google contacts?

In the sidebar navigation, click on the "Sync Contacts" option to instantly import and sync your Google connections into the NxChat ecosystem.
